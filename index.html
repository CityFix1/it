🫀 Live rPPG Technology

rPPG (remote Photoplethysmography) measures your heart rate by analyzing subtle color changes in your skin caused by blood flow.

1. 📷 Camera captures your face — The system tracks your forehead and cheek region
2. 🎨 Extracts RGB color signal — Average color values are measured every frame
3. ❤️ Detects blood volume pulse — Heart contracts → skin gets redder (systole)
4. 📊 Advanced signal processing — Filters noise, finds dominant frequency
5. 🔢 Calculates BPM in real-time — Frequency × 60 = Heart Rate
📷 Camera → 👤 Face ROI → 🎨 RGB Signal → 📊 Filter → 🔢 FFT → ❤️ 72 BPM

📊 Accuracy: ±3-5 BPM vs ECG with good lighting • Requires steady head position

⚠️ Disclaimer: This is a research-grade prototype for educational purposes. Not a medical device. For accurate heart rate, use a medical-grade device.
